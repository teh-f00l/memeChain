How to Play

This game requires you to talk using memes! At their root, memes are cultural ideas, actions, or mannerisms that are passed on with the hope of spreading. You must actively steer the direction that these memes take in our culture by posting your own! Using the Glorious Trainwrecks forum as a site, you will post memes, comment on them when deciding if they are interesting, and post your own in response! We have inspiration images as starting points for the image that you will post.
